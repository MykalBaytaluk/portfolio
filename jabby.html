Jabberwocky

Logo Design & Brand Guideline

Jabberwocky is a venue of intricacy that inspires intrigue and then fulfills it.

A cabinet of curiosities that highlights cocktails, raw-vegan dishes and an undeniably addictive atmosphere.

A cabinet of curiosities that highlights cocktails, raw-vegan dishes and an undeniably addictive atmosphere.

The project at hand is to design a strong icon that will embody this entity and help to be a part of establishing a brand with follow through.

With the elegant decor being clean and tradi­tional, all aspects of this establishment will have a chance to stand out and be discovered in their own, well deserved light. The era of inspiration for the direction of the interior design is one that stands the test of time, time and time again.

This era inspires design on so many levels such as interior design, industrial design and graphic design. The conquest of the Jabberwocky by Lewis Carrol is one of backwards excitement and whimsy; completely reflected in the feel of this brand, said with the utmost admiration.

This word mark allows for just enough intrigue while still being legible. Saving the majority of the letters in the correct order allows for the flow to remain as if the word was written correctly; leaving the few “easter-eggs” (backwards letters) to be found at a second glance.

These treats in the word-mark are an homage to all of the hidden intricacies to be installed and then later found inside of the establishment. One final tie from the word-mark to the timeless design of Jabberwocky is that its influences are a mix between modern and traditional serif typography.

This mark has a playful air that isn’t too serious, one that allows for comfort and understanding yet is still sinister and intriguing.

This icon, which is close enough to a character to be approachable but strong enough to be a mark, is your guide through the world that is Union613, the Speakeasy, and Jabberwocky.
